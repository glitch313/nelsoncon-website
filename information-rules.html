Information & Rules

Event Highlights

  • Close to Dollywood for adventures and rides.
  • Pool and hot tub for daytime and late-night soaking.
  • Many sport amenities such as mini golf course, lit pickleball, indoor basketball court, duckpin bowling alley.
  • A very great living area space and a theater room great for large hangouts.
  • More events to come!

Attendee Rules

  1. Do not use the elevator. If it breaks, they have to send someone out and there is a $500 fee. Whoever breaks it and gets stuck in it will be paying that fee, so it is best to not use it.
  2. Please try not to move around furniture. This happens every year and we always forget where everything originally goes, including beds.
  3. There is a 10 PM curfew for outdoor noise. Make an effort to bring drunk/loud people inside after 10, and no music outside at night. Quiet chilling outdoors is still fine.

Achievements

Earn Nelsoncon achievements throughout the event. Achievements are worth point values and help track your progress across challenges and activities.

Post and track achievements at play.nelsoncon.com
