Nelsoncon Memories

Nelsoncon

The orgin of Nelsoncon at Michael Nelsons house. A 10 day neverending con while his parents were away.

Nelsoncon 2

Airbnb at Bryce Mountain, VA. Slept about 20 people in an 8 person cabin. :p First time for a lot of non locals.

Nelsoncon 3

Cancelation the day before had us scramble into a ski resort cabin at Hidden Valley for 4 days. Was still very enjoyable with a great social area.

Nelsoncon 4

The Bluegrass Palace in Langly Kentucky. Huge leap from previous years! Bigger venue, bigger budget, and more attendees. We had around 32 people this year from all over the USA. Volleyball!

Check out our short film shot at NC4 Juul Man!!

Nelsoncon 5

Back at Bluegrass with our Largest Nelsoncon to date with 40 attendees. It was a lot to handle but still turned out good. Had lights for nighttime volleyball but people drank and partied to hard to acutally use them :p.

Nelsoncon 6

Last year at Bluegrass for 10 days to celebrate Nelsoncon 5 milestone. Nelsoncon Survivor!! While controversal at the time engulfing the vacation for many, produced some great video content and still content yet to come (MICHAEL EDIT THE VIDEO).

Check out Nelsoncon 6 Survivor!!

Nelsoncon 7

Nelsoncon 7 brought us to Frankfort Kentucky. Amazing outdoor enviroment balanced by HORRIBLE sleeping arrangments. Late cancellation didn't give us many options. Enjoyed lots of beer die and ultimate frisbee this year with the large yard and pool hangout area.

Nelsoncon 8

Switching up the venue again we arrived in Charleston WV. This year the focus was on the achievement system made by Garrett that awarded points for completing milestones. A lot of pickleball was played here with the two courts we created outside.
