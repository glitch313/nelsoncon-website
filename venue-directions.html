Venue & Directions

Host Venue

Nelson Civic Center
245 Harbor Line Avenue, Nelson City, NC 27844

Doors open at 8:00 AM. Badge check and guest services are located in the east lobby.

Getting Here

  • Driving: Use I-74, Exit 16. Follow signs for Civic Center District.
  • Transit: Route N3 and N7 stop one block from the venue.
  • Rideshare: Drop-off is at Gate B.
  • Parking: Garage A (paid) and Lot C (free weekend overflow).

Map Reference

Coordinates: 35.7796, -78.6382

Tip: Arrive at least 30 minutes early for morning check-in.
