Nelsoncon Winter 2

Out west for the first time to Salt Lake City Utah! We stayed in the city and went to mountains such as Brighton, Solitude, and Snowbird. Conditions could be better but it was still a great time.

Nelsoncon Winter 3

Out west to Colorado! We hit Keystone, Copper mountain, and Arapahoe Basin. Abasin is many attendees favorite resort they've been to. Medium mountain with great variety and smoothness. The Goat Soup restaurant was also a favorite for this trip.

Nelsoncon Winter 4

Back to Snowshoe for a local trip with a large group of 16! Conditions were super cold and tough to traverse. So nobody actually went to ski at the resort and we just had some parties at the lodge :p
